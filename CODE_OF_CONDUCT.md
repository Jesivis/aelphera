# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

As contributors and maintainers of Aelphera, we pledge to respect all people who contribute through reporting issues, posting feature requests, updating documentation, submitting pull requests, and other activities.

Our standards
- Be respectful and welcoming
- Be considerate and collaborative
- Refrain from demeaning or exclusionary behavior

Reporting
If you are subject to or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the maintainers at contact@aelphera.example (replace with real contact) or open a confidential issue.

Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported and will be reviewed by the project maintainers.
